> ZkVanguard is an AI-powered risk management platform built natively on Sui (with bridges to Cronos, Sepolia, and Hedera). Our flagship product is a database-backed **USDC Community Pool** that auto-allocates deposits across BTC (30%), ETH (30%), SUI (25%), and CRO (15%), executing atomic multi-DEX swaps through the **Cetus aggregator** (Cetus, DeepBook, Turbos, BlueFin, FlowX, Aftermath).
>
> Seven specialized AI agents — Lead, Risk, Hedging, Settlement, Reporting, PriceMonitor, and SuiPool — coordinate autonomously to deliver predictive defense: instead of reacting after a crash, ZkVanguard ingests **Polymarket / Delphi prediction-market signals**, correlates them with the user's portfolio, and auto-hedges *before* the move. Hedges are executed privately via **stealth addresses + ZK commitments**, so on-chain observers cannot infer position sizes or assets.
>
> Privacy is backed by **521-bit ZK-STARK proofs** (CUDA-accelerated, no trusted setup) and verifiable hedge ownership via wallet or proof hash. The Sui side ships with sponsored transactions for gasless UX, a 4-asset rebalancing engine triggered by 5% drift or 75%+ AI confidence, and a full E2E test suite (9 suites / 72 checks) covering aggregator quotes, on-chain reads, commitment schemes, auto-hedging, and the USDC pool API.
>
> Live at https://zkvanguard.xyz. Sui mainnet contracts deployed and operational.
